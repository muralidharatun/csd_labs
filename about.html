About Us

Who We Are

At CSD LABS, we believe that success is defined by the positive impact which we make on the world around us. We believe that success is a journey but not a destination. Since CSD LABS being established in 2012 to the present day, our goal has always been to provide access to affordable services to thousands of customers who are in need. To accomplish this vision, we have sought to be an innovative leader in the development, synthesizing, manufacturing and marketing of fine chemicals/reference standards/Impurities metabolites/Intermediates of API’s/phyto-chemical reference standards/Natural products/aromatic/heterocyclic/amino acids/carbohydrates/catalysts/reagents etc and active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), intermediates.

Why Choose Us?

Through state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, visionary leadership, and a staff of dedicated and experienced employees, CSD LABS continuously strives to offer best services who are in need.. In order to achieve this goal, we relentlessly focus on offering the highest levels of quality, excellence and trust to our pharmaceutical partners, doctors, patients and the society in general and by providing pharmaceutical products at an affordable cost. Fundamentally, we value health and prosperity of all. Our passion for improving quality of life extends beyond our business and transcends everything we do. We continuously seek opportunities to help the society through our corporate responsibility initiatives. We continue to support with several health programmes, financial support, educational and welfare activities in communities surrounding the company’s factories by providing healthcare, education, scholarships and community infrastructure.

From the products we produce, to the pharmaceutical partners we support, to the patients we help; CSD LABS is committed to being the change we want to see in the world.
